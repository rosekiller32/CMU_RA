LOCAL WORKFORCE DEVELOPMENT BOARD GRIEVANCE POLICY

Partner4Work (formerly the Three Rivers Workforce Investment Board) has established and maintains a grievance procedure for grievances or complaints about its programs and activities. All applicants and participants of the employment and training programs offered by the PARTNER4WORK are entitled to take advantage of this procedure. Initially you should attempt to resolve your complaint informally at the agency/department/ training institution where you are placed. If the complaint cannot be resolved at this level to your satisfaction, you may file a complaint in writing summarizing your issue to: Compliance Manager Partner4Work 650 Smithfield Street, Suite 2600 Pittsburgh, PA 15222 Compliance Manager reviews and/or investigates the complaint in an attempt to reach an informal resolution. If an informal resolution cannot be reached, a formal hearing will be provided within 30 days of hearing.
