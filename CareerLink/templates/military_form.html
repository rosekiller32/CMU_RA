Military Section

If you served in the US Military, please complete this section.

Priority of Service (POS) is extended to eligible Veterans* and covered spouses** to receive preference for all Workforce Development services offered through PA CareerLink®, including resources in our Career Resource Center. POS customers, regardless of when they apply for a limited resource, will be moved to the top of the list ahead of non‐POS customers for the available resource or program. CUSTOMERS MAY BE ELIGIBLE FOR PRIORITY OF SERVICE IF:
1. *They served on active duty for at least 180 days (after training; or were medically discharged during training) in the United States military (Army, Navy, Marines, Air Force, Coast Guard) or were activated and received a campaign badge as a member of a National Guard or Reserve unit and were not dishonorably discharged; or
2. **They are the spouse of a veteran who is a Prisoner of War, Missing in Action, is 100% Disabled or has died from a service‐related disability
